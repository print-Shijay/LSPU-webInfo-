New Licensed Psychometricians

October 2025 

The College of Arts and Sciences (CAS) of Laguna State Polytechnic University (LSPU) posted a commendable performance in the September 2025 Board 
Licensure Examination for Psychometricians, achieving an overall institutional passing rate of 76.79%.
Leading the university’s success was Kathryn Christel Joy R. Dela Rosa from the LSPU-Santa Cruz Campus,
 who secured 10th place among the top examinees with a rating of 87.60%.
The performance of LSPU per campus is as follows:
- LSPU–Santa Cruz Campus: 73.02% (92 passers out of 126 examinees)
- LSPU–Siniloan Campus: 78.95% (60 passers out of 76 examinees)
- LSPU–San Pablo City Campus: 84.21% (48 passers out of 57 examinees)
- LSPU–Los Baños Campus: 73.53% (25 passers out of 34 examinees)
LSPU’s strong showing in this year’s licensure examination reflects the university’s continued commitment to 
academic excellence and professional preparedness in the field of psychology.
Congratulations to our new psychometricians!
