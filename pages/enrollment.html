ADMISSION GUIDELINES

First Semester A.Y. 2026-2027

Welcome to the Laguna State Polytechnic University!

We’re glad you're considering becoming part of the LSPU community. The Admission Office, in coordination with our colleges and other concerned units, is pleased to share the official admission guidelines for the First Semester of Academic Year 2026–2027.

Please read through the information carefully to guide you through your application.

Admission Link:https://admissions.lspusys.com

Admission Requirements

College Qualification

General Weighted Average (GWA)

As stipulated in the LSPU Handbook, colleges may set qualifications on the general weighted average (GWA) of applicants to be admitted to each program (board or non-board) in consideration of accommodation capacity and standards.

Documentary Requirements

For First-Year College Applicants

  1. Recent 2x2 ID picture (white background)
  2. Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character (with certification of current grade level & school year)
  4. Certified True Copy of SF9 (Grade 11)

For Transfer Student Applicants

  1. Recent 2x2 ID picture (white background)
  2. Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character (with certification of current year level & school year)
  4. Certified true copy of grades / transcript
Reminder: No original documentary requirements shall be physically submitted during admission. Present authenticated or certified true copies only.

Step-by-Step Enrollment Process

Step 1
Online Application. Accomplish the LSPU Online Application. Make sure to input accurate information in all required fields.
Step 2
Examination Schedule. Check your email for the examination schedule sent by the Admission/Guidance Office. Ensure your email address is correct and active.
Step 3
Admission Examination. Take the exam at your designated campus testing room. Bring your printed application form and certified documents for verification.
Step 4
Interview Schedule. Watch for the interview schedule email from your chosen college or program coordinator.
Step 5
Interview. Attend the scheduled interview with the college dean or designated faculty.
Step 6
Posting of Results. Results will be posted by the Admission/Guidance Office and on official LSPU social channels.
Step 7
Medical Examination. Secure the required medical certificate (drug test & X-ray) and submit results as instructed.
Step 8
Enrollment. Proceed with enrollment as per the announced schedule. Follow payment and registration instructions.
